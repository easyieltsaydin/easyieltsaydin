Teaching English Based on Your Personal Psychology

IELTS is not simply a language exam. It is an assessment of how effectively you organize academic ideas, manage time pressure, control grammatical accuracy, and express meaning clearly under exam conditions. Because of this, Aydin Mahmoudi’s teaching philosophy focuses first on the student — not the textbook.

Every learner has a unique cognitive and emotional structure. Some students require strong frameworks, others need flexibility. Some struggle with confidence, others with organization. Lessons are adapted to your personal psychology so progress becomes natural, efficient, and sustainable.

What You Will Learn

Academic IELTS Writing

Master Task 1 and Task 2 through advanced structure models, logical idea generation systems, paragraph cohesion strategies, grammatical accuracy control, and examiner-level feedback designed for Band 7–9 performance.

Academic IELTS Speaking

Develop fluency, coherent expansion, pronunciation clarity, lexical flexibility, and confidence through real examiner-style questions and psychology-driven speaking drills.

Advanced Academic Grammar

Learn how grammar truly affects band scores and how to use complex sentence structures accurately without risking band-limiting mistakes.

Vocabulary & Band 7–9 Strategies

Build topic-based academic vocabulary, paraphrasing mastery, collocation awareness, and strategic word choice without memorization overload or unnatural language use.
