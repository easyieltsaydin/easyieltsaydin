Courses & Programs

Each course is designed around your goals, psychology, and current level. Whether you are preparing for IELTS or developing academic communication skills, the focus is on real language growth, not temporary tricks.

IELTS Preparation

Academic IELTS

Designed for university admission and academic migration purposes. This course focuses on examiner expectations, structured writing, precise grammar control, and high-level academic vocabulary.

  • Writing Task 1 (Reports & Graphs)
  • Writing Task 2 (Academic Essays)
  • Speaking Band 7–9 strategies
  • Grammar accuracy & coherence
  • Exam mindset & time control

General IELTS

Ideal for immigration and everyday communication goals. Emphasis is placed on clarity, natural language use, and practical writing tasks required by the General Training module.

  • Letter Writing (Formal & Informal)
  • Essay Writing (Opinion & Problem-Solution)
  • Speaking fluency & confidence
  • Grammar for real-life accuracy
  • Score improvement strategies

Academic & Communication Skills

Academic Writing

A non-IELTS course designed for students who need to write clearly and formally at university level. Focus is placed on argument development, clarity, and logical structure.

  • Essay and paragraph structure
  • Thesis statements & cohesion
  • Formal academic tone
  • Critical thinking in writing
  • Editing and self-correction

Speaking Skills

This course focuses on fluency, pronunciation, and confidence. It is suitable for academic, professional, and everyday communication needs outside of test preparation.

  • Fluency & natural pacing
  • Pronunciation & clarity
  • Confidence building
  • Real-life speaking practice
  • Vocabulary activation
